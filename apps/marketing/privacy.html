Privacy

Privacy at a glance

The full privacy policy is being drafted for legal review and will be published here before public launch. This page summarises the commitments the product is built around — they are enforced in the architecture, not just promised in prose.

The complete, counsel-reviewed policy will live at docs/legal/privacy.md. Until it ships, this summary describes how Undertone handles your data today.

Your audio

Your audio is transcribed in real time and discarded — it never touches our disks. There is no server-side code path that writes your recordings to storage. If a dictation can't reach our servers, the audio is buffered only on your own machine and retried, so your words are never silently lost.

Your transcripts

History is encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM; plaintext never hits our storage. Search works over an encrypted index, so we can find your words for you without keeping them readable on disk. You can delete any entry, or your whole history, at any time.

Telemetry

Usage analytics count words and measure latency. They never carry what you said. Analytics are anonymous by default and can be turned off entirely in settings.

Questions

Reach us at privacy@undertone.example. This summary is informational and will be superseded by the published policy.
