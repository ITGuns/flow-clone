Using Undertone

Undertone is a dictation tool for your own writing. You keep ownership of everything you dictate. Use it lawfully and don't attempt to disrupt the service or work around plan limits for others.

Plans and billing

The Free plan is available at no cost. Pro is a paid subscription billed monthly or yearly, with a 14-day trial that requires no card. You can cancel at any time and keep Pro through the end of the period you've paid for.

Fair use

Pro is unlimited for ordinary dictation, subject to a weekly fair-use cap that exists to protect service quality. We'll always tell you where you stand rather than cutting you off without warning.

Changes

We may update these terms as the product evolves; material changes will be communicated before they take effect. Continued use after an update means you accept the revised terms.
