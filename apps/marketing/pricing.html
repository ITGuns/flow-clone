Questions

Pricing, answered

What counts as a "formatted word"?

We count the words in the polished text Undertone actually inserts at your cursor, measured when the text is produced. Filler you spoke but that never gets typed — "um", false starts, "scratch that" — doesn't count against you.

What happens on Free when I hit 2,000 words?

Dictation pauses until the weekly counter resets, and Undertone tells you plainly rather than silently dropping your words. Your last transcript is still handed back so nothing you said is lost. Weeks reset every Monday.

Do I need a credit card to try Pro?

No. The 14-day Pro trial starts the moment you sign up, with no card and no commitment. If you do nothing when it ends, you simply drop back to the Free plan.

Is Pro really unlimited? What is the 50k cap?

Pro is unlimited for normal human dictation. The 50k-words-per-week fair-use cap exists only to stop automated or abusive use from degrading the service for everyone else — a genuinely heavy speaker won't come close.

Can I switch between monthly and yearly?

Yes. You can move from $12/mo to $96/yr (or back) at any time, and the change is prorated against what you've already paid. Yearly works out to two months free.

Does the price include both macOS and Windows?

One subscription covers you on every device you use, on both macOS and Windows. Your dictionary and history sync across all of them.
