# Legal drafts

These are **drafts, not final legal documents**. `privacy-policy.md` and `terms-of-service.md`
were drafted on **2026-07-15** and **require review by qualified legal counsel before any public
launch**. Every technical claim in the privacy policy was written to match what the code actually
does as of the drafting date (audio not persisted, transcripts AES-256-GCM encrypted at rest with
a keyed-hash search index, anonymous content-free telemetry) — if the implementation changes, the
policy must be updated to stay true. The company name is a placeholder (`[COMPANY]`, product
codename "Undertone"), and all jurisdiction-, regulatory-, and liability-specific items are marked
`[COUNSEL: …]` for counsel to complete.
